Chicken with rice-noodles

Description

asian style chicken pot meal with rice noodles, vegetables and delicious soy-tahin sauce

Incredients for 4 Portions

  • 500 g. chicken breast
  • 2 table spoons of Olive oil
  • 400 g. red cabbage
  • 400 g. carrots
  • 200 g. Reice-noodles
  • 4 Table spoons of Soy sauce
  • 3 Table spoons of Water
  • 2 Table spoons of Tahin
  • 1 Table spoon of chopped ginger
  • 2 Cloves of garlic
  • 30 g. of coriander
  • 1 tea spoon Chili flakes
  • 1 tea spoon Salt
  • 4 Table spoons of spring onions
  • 2 Table spoons of sesame seeds
  • 1 Table spoons red chili pod, wedges cut

Steps

  1. Cut chicken breasts in small pieces
  2. add some Olive oil to a pan and fry chicken on medium heat until golden
  3. add garlic, ginger, chili flakes and salt
  4. cut a cabbage into strips and grate carrots
  5. Cook noodles and put them into a cold water after that
  6. mix soz sauce with Tahin and water to form a homogeneous Dressing
  7. mit the noodles with carrots, cabbage and fried chicken
  8. serve with the dressing and coriander
