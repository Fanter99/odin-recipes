Feta-Broccoli Salad

Description

Fresh delicious salad which you can enjoy cold or warm

ingredients for 1 Portion

  • 70 g. braun rice
  • 200 g. Broccoli
  • 40 g. Baby Spinach
  • 1/2 tea spoon of olive oil
  • 50 g. Feta cheese

Steps

  1. Cook rice
  2. give some Olive oil to the pan, also add broccoli and spinach, season with salt, pepper and chilli if you want to
  3. mix the seard vegetables with cooked rice
  4. garnish with feta cheese
