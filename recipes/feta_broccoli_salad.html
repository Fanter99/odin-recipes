Feta-Broccoli Salad

Description

Fresh delicious salad, which you can enjoy cold or warm

Incredients for 1 Portion

  • 70 g. braun rice
  • 200 g. Broccoli
  • 40 g. Baby Spinach
  • 1/2 tea spoon of Olive oil
  • 50 g. Feta cheese

Steps

  1. Cook rice
  2. give some Olive oil to the pan, also add broccoli and spinach, season wiht salt, pepper and chilli if you want
  3. mix the seard vegetables with cooked rice
  4. garnish with feta cheese
