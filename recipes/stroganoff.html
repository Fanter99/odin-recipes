Stroganoff chicken

Description

russian style chicken with rice and Ajvar sauce

Incredients for 1 Portion

  • 120 g. chicken breast
  • 1/2 tea spoon Olive oil
  • 3 Table spoons Ajvar
  • 80 g. of curd
  • 50 g. Onion
  • 60 g. rice
  • 10 g. of vegetable stock cubes

Steps

  1. Cook rice
  2. cut the chicken into strips and slice onion
  3. add Olive oil to a pan and fry chicken and onion
  4. add ajvar, curd and a stock cube. let everything simmer and season with salt and pepper
  5. serve schicken with rice
